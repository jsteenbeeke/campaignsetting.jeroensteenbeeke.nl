Outwardly presenting themselves as followers of the god [Xax](/pantheons/the_unscathed/xax). The Heralds of Passion are a recently revealed group of demon-worshippers who seek the liberation of one or more demon princes.

## Beliefs

1. The gods have failed us
2. The Great Cataclysm could have been prevented
3. Passion is the ultimate expression of freedom

## Goals

To harness the power of the demon princes to undo the damage of the Great Cataclysm
