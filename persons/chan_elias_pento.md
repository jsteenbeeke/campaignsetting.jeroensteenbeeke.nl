Chan Elias Pento was the former ambassador to the [Consensus Council](/organizations/consensus_council) for the [Chadori Republic](/nations/chadori_republic). A former member of the [Council of Voices](/nations/chadori_republic#government), he was one of the few remaining Chadori who took part in the slave rebellion against the [Naderi Empire](/nations/naderi_empire) on [Danaros](/locations/danaros), and was one of the founding fathers of the Republic.

Despite his advanced age he was still quite vital, and was considered a moderating influence against the expansionistic tendencies of his people.

He was assassinated on [6th Nêlanië 55 NR](/general/calendar) by Nim Beijor, an agent of the **Heralds of Passion**. Initially his death was blamed on [Melindra Lonaria](/persons/melindra_lonaria), though evidence was later presented by a group of adventurers calling themselves "Off by one" that she was mind-controlled by Nim Beijor.
