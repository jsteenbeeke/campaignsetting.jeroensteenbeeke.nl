Andir Kan is the [Chadori](/nations/chadori_republic) ambassador to the [Consensus Council](/organizations/consensus_council). He gained this position after the assassination of the previous ambassador [Chan Elias Pento](/persons/chan_elias_pento).

Andir was formerly a logistics officer for the **Unbound Fleet**, but was asked personally by Chan Elias Pento to serve as his assistant with the intent to replace him in due time.
